## Verdict

**Version A (PPTX v3) wins** due to superior visual design with color-coded sections, better figure presentation with descriptive captions, more comprehensive speaker notes including timing guidance, and stronger audience engagement elements. The color differentiation between client-side (blue), engine-side (orange), and theory (grey) sections creates a clear visual structure that aids understanding. While Version B offers excellent portability as HTML, Version A provides a more polished, academic presentation better suited for a 25-minute group meeting.
