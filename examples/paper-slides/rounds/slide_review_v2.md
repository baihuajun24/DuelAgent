## Verdict

**Version B (HTML) wins this round.** Version B delivers a more polished visual experience with a professionally designed CSS layout (progress bars, color-coded step cards, callout boxes, formula blocks, and code syntax highlighting), a dedicated background/pipeline slide that improves narrative scaffolding, and two appendix slides that add depth without cluttering the main flow. Version A remains strong on editability (native PPTX is easier to tweak in PowerPoint/Keynote) and has solid speaker notes, but its visual design and information hierarchy do not yet match Version B's level of polish.
